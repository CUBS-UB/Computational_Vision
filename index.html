Welcome to CSE 673 Computational Vision¶

This is an advanced course in computer science, that will introduce students to modern computer vision techniques. We will analyze how recent advances in deep learning methods have impacted the area of computer vision. The course will cover topics starting from the basics of deep learning to state-of-the-art methods used in real-world computer vision systems.
